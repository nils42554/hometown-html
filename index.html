Top three activities to do at Whetstone

Abandon your fears on the pristine slopes!

The slopes are a stones throw away and you can even leave your little ones at the creche.

Go for a picnic in the local park

Friary park is perfect for picnic, getting a tan or just chillin'

Ride the wave.

The Whetstone ocean can get pretty funky, catch the surf and a sunset.
